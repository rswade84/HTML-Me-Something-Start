Panama City Beach, FL

Our amazing beach vacation in Panama City Beach! We enjoyed the sun, sand, pools, and waves.
The clear waters and white sandy beaches were absolutely breathtaking.
We built sandcastles, tried some delicious seafood, and created tons of unforgettable memories.

Chicago, IL

Our exciting city adventure in Chicago! We explored the Windy City's iconic landmarks.
From the stunning views at Willis Tower to the reflective Bean at Millennium Park,
we also got to check out the Home Alone house in Winnetka!

Branson, MO

Our fun-filled trip to Branson! We enjoyed the perfect blend of entertainment and nature.
We watched incredible shows, rode a few roller coasters at Silver Dollar City,
and ended with a father son float trip down White River. A perfect family getaway!
